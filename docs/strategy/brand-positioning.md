# Brand Positioning — Hart Family Dental

## Positioning statement

Hart Family Dental is the approachable High Desert and Coachella Valley family practice that makes everyday dental care comfortable — and advanced treatment (implants, restorative, and dentist-supervised straightening) clear, honest, and within reach.

## Personality

Friendly · Trustworthy · Family-oriented · Modern · Accessible · Professional · Comfortable · Nonjudgmental · Transparent · Community-focused · Capable

## Message hierarchy

1. **Brand:** Hart Family Dental  
2. **Promise:** Care that feels personal — solutions that restore confidence  
3. **Proof:** Two convenient locations, experienced dentist leadership, clear next steps, financing options when approved  
4. **Ask:** Call or request an appointment

## Core narrative (approved tone)

We welcome patients who feel nervous, overdue, or unsure where to start. Whether you need a checkup, help with a dental emergency, or want to explore implants or a straighter smile under dentist supervision, we explain options in plain language and help you take the next step.

## Differentiation (claim only what is verified)

Safe to emphasize once confirmed:

- Two local offices (Yucca Valley + Desert Hot Springs)  
- Family practice feel with advanced treatment pathways  
- Cash-pay and financing education (partners TBD)  
- Spanish / language support — **only if verified**  
- Same-week emergency availability — **only if operationally true**

Avoid until verified:

- “Best dentist,” “#1 implants,” guaranteed results  
- Specific prices or “free” exams without written offer terms  
- Services not offered at that location  
- DIY / mail-order orthodontics without dentist oversight

## Voice examples

| Do | Don’t |
| --- | --- |
| “Let’s find a plan that fits your smile and your budget.” | “Guaranteed perfect smile in 6 months.” |
| “Implant consultations with clear next steps.” | “Pain-free forever / never fail implants.” |
| “Dentist-supervised straightening — not DIY.” | “Straighten teeth entirely from home with no visits.” |
| “New patients welcome at both offices.” | Invented testimonials or stock “patient stories” as real |

## Visual direction (website)

High-desert modern: open sky blues, warm stone neutrals, deep sage accent — not generic purple SaaS, not cream/terracotta cliché, not dark-mode dental gloom. Photography priority: real offices, team, welcoming treatment rooms (with photo releases).
