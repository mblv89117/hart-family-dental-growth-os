# Campaign Foundation — Dentist-Supervised Teeth Straightening

## Product principle
Accessible, budget-conscious, **dentist-supervised** straightening — never unsupervised DIY orthodontics. No automated diagnosis, prescription, or approval.

## Journey
1. Educational LP `/teeth-straightening`  
2. Preliminary smile assessment  
3. Photo upload (if enabled)  
4. Eligibility questions  
5. Consult scheduling  
6. In-office scan / X-ray / exam / records as required  
7. Dentist review & approval  
8. Pricing & financing transparency  
9. Mail delivery or office pickup when permitted  
10. Instructions  
11. Progress monitoring  
12. Retainers & follow-up  
13. Escalation to in-person care when needed  

## Brand working title (for approval)
**Hart Straight** or **Hart Smile Align** — final name TBD with Harry Hart / Lindsay.

## Launch gates
- Clinical workflow signed off  
- Liability / consent reviewed  
- Pricing approved  
- Front-desk script live  
- Lead routing SLA met  

## Paid/social
Hold until Phase 1 tracking + clinical gates complete.
