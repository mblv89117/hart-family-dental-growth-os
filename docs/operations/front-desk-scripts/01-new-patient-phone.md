# New-Patient Phone Script

**Goal:** Book the right appointment at the right office. Warm, unhurried, clear.

## Opening
“Thank you for calling Hart Family Dental, [Yucca Valley / Desert Hot Springs]. This is [Name]. How can I help you today?”

## Discover
- New or existing patient?  
- Which office is more convenient?  
- What brings them in (checkup, tooth pain, implants, smile, etc.)?  
- Urgency / pain level?  
- Preferred days/times?  
- Insurance vs cash-pay interest?  

## If emergency / pain
- Empathize; do not diagnose  
- Offer soonest emergency evaluation slot  
- Collect callback number; confirm they can come in  

## Book
“I can reserve [day/time] at our [office] for a new-patient visit. We’ll [briefly state what’s included per approved policy]. Does that work?”

## Confirm
- Name, DOB, phone, email  
- Office address  
- Arrival time / forms  
- What to bring (ID, insurance card, med list)  
- SMS/email reminder consent  

## Close
“You’re all set for [datetime] at [office], [address]. If anything changes, call us at [phone]. We’re glad you’re coming in.”

## If no slot
Offer alternate office or waitlist; never leave without a next step.
