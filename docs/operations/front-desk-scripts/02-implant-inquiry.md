# Implant Inquiry Script

**Goal:** Book an implant consultation or second-opinion visit. Do not quote guaranteed outcomes or unauthorized prices.

## Opening
“I’d be happy to help you explore implant options. Are you hoping to replace one tooth, several teeth, or looking at a fuller smile restoration?”

## Clarify
- Missing / failing teeth? Denture wearer?  
- Prior implant consult elsewhere (second opinion)?  
- Preferred office?  
- Timeline and budget concerns?  
- Financing interest?  

## Position the consult
“The next step is a consultation so the dentist can examine your mouth, review imaging if needed, and explain which options may fit you. We’ll outline clear next steps — no pressure.”

## Do / Don’t
| Do | Don’t |
| --- | --- |
| Book consult | Promise “you’re a candidate” |
| Mention financing education if approved | Invent monthly payments |
| Explain cash-pay / phased possibilities in general terms | Guarantee success or lifetime results |
| Offer either office if capacity differs | Diagnose over the phone |

## Close
Confirm appointment, office, what to expect, and that a specialist referral may be recommended if clinically appropriate (only if true for practice).
