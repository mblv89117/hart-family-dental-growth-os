# Missed-Call & Voicemail Scripts

## Missed-call callback
“Hi [Name], this is [Name] from Hart Family Dental in [Office]. We saw we missed your call and wanted to make sure we could help. You can reach us at [phone], or I’ll try you again [time]. If this is a dental emergency, please call us back as soon as you can.”

## Voicemail (office greeting — customize per location)
“You’ve reached Hart Family Dental in [Yucca Valley / Desert Hot Springs]. Our office is currently [with patients / closed]. Please leave your name, number, and a brief reason for your call. For dental emergencies, leave a message and we will return urgent calls as soon as possible during business hours. You can also request an appointment at hartfamilydds.com. Thank you.”

## After-hours auto-ack (SMS/email)
“Thanks for contacting Hart Family Dental. We’ve received your message outside office hours. We’ll follow up on the next business day. If you have a serious emergency, seek urgent medical/dental care as appropriate.”
