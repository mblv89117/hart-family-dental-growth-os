# Additional Sequences (Front Desk + Lifecycle)

## No-show recovery
1. Same day: call + SMS “We missed you — want to reschedule?”  
2. Day 2: call  
3. Day 5: email with easy booking CTA  

## Unscheduled treatment follow-up
“Hi [Name], this is [Name] from Hart Family Dental. Dr. [Name] reviewed options for [general description — no sensitive detail in SMS]. I’m calling to answer questions and help with scheduling or financing education when you’re ready.”

## Consultation confirmation
24–48h before: confirm time, address, parking, forms, bring list. Offer reschedule path.

## Consultation reminder
Morning-of SMS if consented.

## Post-consultation
Same day or next: thank you + “questions about your plan?” + financing offer if appropriate. No clinical details in marketing SMS.

## Review request (eligible, non-incentivized)
“Thanks for visiting Hart Family Dental. If you had a good experience, here’s a link to share feedback on Google: [link]. Concerns? Call us directly at [phone] so we can help.”

## Referral request
Only after positive experience; no paid review schemes.

## Inactive reactivation
“It’s been a while since your last visit at Hart Family Dental. When you’re ready for a checkup or have a dental concern, we’re here at [offices/phones]. Reply or call to schedule.”
