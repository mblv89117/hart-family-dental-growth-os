# Teeth-Straightening Inquiry Script

**Goal:** Book assessment / consult. Emphasize **dentist-supervised** care — never DIY.

## Key line
“Our straightening program is dentist-supervised. It is not do-it-yourself orthodontics. You’ll complete a preliminary assessment online or by phone, then we’ll schedule the records and exam the dentist needs before any treatment is approved.”

## Steps to explain
1. Preliminary questions / smile photos (if used)  
2. Consultation / exam / scans / X-rays as required  
3. Dentist review and approval  
4. Transparent pricing / financing discussion  
5. Aligners via approved delivery or office pickup when appropriate  
6. Progress monitoring and retainers  
7. In-person visit if concerns arise  

## Booking
Offer smile assessment appointment or phone follow-up within SLA. Collect office preference and consent for SMS/email.

## Red flags → in-person sooner
Severe pain, swelling, trauma, gum disease concerns, medical complexity — route to clinical evaluation, not an online-only path.
