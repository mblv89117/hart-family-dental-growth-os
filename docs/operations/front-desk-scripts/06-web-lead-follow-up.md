# Web Lead Follow-Up Script

## Call 1 (immediate)
“Hi [Name], this is [Name] from Hart Family Dental. You requested information about [service] for our [office] office. Do you have a quick minute so I can get you scheduled?”

## If no answer → SMS (if consented)
“Hi [Name], Hart Family Dental here — we received your [appointment / implant / smile] request. Reply YES for a callback or call [phone].”

## Email follow-up
Subject: We received your request — Hart Family Dental  
Body: Thank them, restate service/office, offer 2–3 appointment windows, link/phone, opt-out for marketing messages.

## Cadence (if not booked)
1. Immediate call + auto email  
2. Same-day SMS/call #2  
3. Next-day call + email  
4. Day 3 value email (education)  
5. Day 7 final soft close  
Stop or slow nurture per consent; never harass.
