# SMS & Email Templates (Consent-Required)

## Consent language (website forms)

**SMS:** By providing your mobile number and checking this box, you agree to receive appointment-related and practice informational texts from Hart Family Dental. Message/data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase or treatment.

**Email:** I agree to receive email communication from Hart Family Dental about appointments, education, and practice updates. I may unsubscribe at any time.

## SMS library

| Code | Template |
| --- | --- |
| SMS-CONFIRM | Hart Family Dental: You’re scheduled [Day, Date] [Time] at [Office], [Short Address]. Reply STOP to opt out. |
| SMS-REMIND | Reminder: Hart Family Dental appointment tomorrow [Time] at [Office]. Call [Phone] to reschedule. |
| SMS-MISSED | We missed your call at Hart Family Dental ([Office]). Call us at [Phone] or reply for a callback. |
| SMS-WEBLEAD | We received your website request. We’ll call shortly from [Phone]. Reply STOP to opt out. |
| SMS-REVIEW | Thank you for visiting Hart Family Dental. Share feedback: [GBP short link]. Questions? Call [Phone]. |

## Email subjects

- We received your appointment request  
- Your implant consultation next steps  
- Dentist-supervised smile assessment — what to expect  
- Friendly reminder: upcoming visit  
- Options for moving forward with treatment  
- It’s easy to restart your dental care  

## Rules

- No PHI or detailed diagnoses in marketing SMS/email  
- Honor STOP/unsubscribe immediately  
- Separate transactional appointment messages from promotional where required by law  
- Store consent timestamps with lead records
