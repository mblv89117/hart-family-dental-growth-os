# Review Generation System

## Goals
Increase review volume and average rating ethically at both locations; route service issues internally first.

## Eligible request timing
After completed positive-leaning visits (hygiene, completed treatment, consult satisfaction) — **do not** selectively request only when legally prohibited “cherry picking” applies; follow California / platform rules and practice policy. Prefer asking all eligible patients via a standard process, with easy path to contact the office for concerns.

## Flow
1. Front desk or automated post-visit message (consented channel)  
2. “How was your visit?” internal pulse (optional)  
3. If concern → call Lindsay / office manager; do not send to public review  
4. If willing → direct link to Google (primary) and optionally Yelp  
5. Log invite sent / completed in tracker  

## Never
- Compensate for positive reviews  
- Write fake reviews  
- Suppress legitimate negatives unlawfully  
- Confirm patient status in public replies  
- Include PHI in responses  

## Response drafts

**Positive:** “Thank you for sharing your experience with Hart Family Dental. Our team is glad to help patients feel comfortable — we appreciate you!”

**Negative:** “Thank you for your feedback. We take concerns seriously and would like the chance to make things right. Please contact our office at [phone] and ask for [manager] so we can help.”

## KPIs
Review count / rating by location / month; invite→review conversion; response rate within 48h; themes for ops improvement.
