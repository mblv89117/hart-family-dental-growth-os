# Lead Response Scripts (Wendy)

Keep warm, short, and honest. Do not diagnose. Do not invent prices.

## Website / Hotmail — first call

“Hi [Name], this is Wendy with Hart Family Dental. You requested information about [service] for our [Yucca Valley / Desert Hot Springs] office. Do you have a quick minute so I can help you get scheduled?”

## No answer — consented SMS

“Hi [Name], Wendy at Hart Family Dental — we received your [service] request for [office]. Reply YES for a callback or call [office phone]. Reply STOP to opt out.”

## Implant priority

“Thanks for reaching out about implants. The next step is a consultation so the dentist can evaluate what’s appropriate. I can offer [two time options] at [office]. Which works better?”

## Clear aligners / smile assessment

“Thanks for the smile assessment. This is only a preliminary step — it doesn’t approve treatment. We’ll schedule records/exam so Dr. Hart can decide what’s appropriate. What days work for [office]?”

## Cash-pay

“We welcome patients paying without insurance. After we understand what you need clinically, we’ll explain options and payment pathways clearly — without pressure.”

## Emergency

“I’m sorry you’re in discomfort. Let’s get you the soonest evaluation we can. Can you come to [office] on [day/time]? If this is a medical emergency, please call 911.”

## Closing a Lost lead

“I understand timing isn’t right. You’re always welcome to call us at [phone] when you’re ready. Take care.”
