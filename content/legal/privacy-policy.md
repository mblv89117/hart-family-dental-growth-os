**Draft for legal review — not final legal advice.**

**Effective date:** TBD (publish on go-live)

## Who we are
Hart Family Dental operates dental offices in Yucca Valley and Desert Hot Springs, California. Contact: see website Contact page or call the office you visit.

## Information we collect
- Contact and appointment request details you submit (name, phone, email, preferred office, service interest, message)
- Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited (analytics)
- Communications you send us

## How we use information
- Respond to appointment and information requests
- Operate and improve our website and patient experience
- Send appointment-related messages and, where you consent, marketing email/SMS
- Comply with law and protect our practice

## PHI / dental records
Clinical dental records are maintained under separate HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices at our offices. This website privacy policy covers marketing site use and form submissions. Do not submit sensitive medical details through marketing forms.

## Sharing
We use service providers (hosting, analytics, communications) under appropriate agreements. We do not sell personal information as commonly defined under applicable law. We may disclose information if required by law.

## SMS/Email
See consent language on forms. Opt out of SMS via STOP; unsubscribe from marketing email via the link provided.

## Retention & security
We retain information as needed for operations and legal requirements. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards; no method of transmission is 100% secure.

## Your choices
Request access/correction of marketing contact data by contacting the practice. Office clinical record rights are governed by HIPAA notices.

## Children
Website not directed to children under 13.

## Changes
We may update this policy; the effective date will change.
